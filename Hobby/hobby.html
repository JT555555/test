Porsche 911 Timeline

The Porsche 911 has evolved over decades, retaining its iconic design and innovation.

  1. 1964: The Birth of the Porsche 911
    • Introduced as the "901" but renamed due to a trademark dispute.
  2. 1973: The Carrera RS 2.7
    • A lightweight, race-ready version of the 911.
  3. 1989: Introduction of the 964
    • Featured all-wheel drive and modernized systems.
  4. 1999: Water-Cooled Revolution
    • The 996 model introduced water-cooled engines.
